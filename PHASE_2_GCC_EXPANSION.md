### âŒ Don't Become a Bank

**Mistake**: "Let's offer savings accounts, lending, remittance"

**Why It Fails**:
- Banking requires massive capital (capital adequacy ratios)
- Heavy regulation (SAMA banking license = 2-3 years)

**Nuqta Approach**: Financial infrastructure (WaaS, controlled BNPL), not full banking.

**Why It Fails**:

- Regulatory scrutiny (SAMA watches consumer debt closely)
- Capital intensive (need balance sheet for lending)

**Nuqta Approach**: BNPL stays <10% of revenue, salary-linked only, education/healthcare/fitness categories only.

---

### âŒ Don't Do Heavy Media Investments

**Mistake**: "Let's become a media company with content studios, influencer agencies"

**Why It Fails**:
- Not core business (Nuqta is payments infrastructure, not media)
- Low margins (media = 20-30% margins vs 60% for SaaS)
- Team distraction (founders become content producers, not platform builders)

**Nuqta Approach**: User-generated content (UGC) stays social bonus-driven, not content studio-produced.

---

### âŒ Don't Build Custom Enterprise Features Per Client

**Mistake**: "Panda wants custom reporting, Jarir wants custom integrations, Albaik wants custom loyalty rules"

**Why It Fails**:
- Product bloat (100 custom features = unmaintainable)
- Engineering distraction (team becomes services, not product)
- Low leverage (build once for one client vs build once for all)

**Nuqta Approach**: Enterprise tier with configurable options, not bespoke builds.
