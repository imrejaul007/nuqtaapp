## üî• INVESTOR OBJECTION HANDLING

### **Q: "What if merchants don't put up QR codes?"**
**A:** "We've already validated this. 30 merchant LOIs signed. They WANT this because:
1. It costs them AED 0 upfront
2. Their competitor will do it if they don't
3. They pay only when it works (customer redeems)

Plus, we make setup brain-dead simple: Scan QR, enter business details, get instant QR code. 5 minutes."

---

### **Q: "Why would a merchant choose you over their own loyalty program?"**
**A:** "We're NOT competing with their loyalty program. We're making it universal.

Example: Cafe has punch card (buy 9, get 1 free). With Nuqta, that AED 10 discount is now redeemable at 500 merchants. Customer earns coins at cafe, spends at gym.

Merchant wins because:
1. Their discount now travels with customer (brand exposure)
2. They tap into 499 other merchants' customer bases
3. Zero tech investment (we build the platform)"

---

### **Q: "What's your merchant retention rate?"**
**A:** "Target: 90% Year 1. Why high?

1. Sunk cost: Once QR is at counter, removing it = losing customers
2. Customer pressure: 'Why did you remove Nuqta?'
3. Network effects: More users = more redemptions = more value

We've seen this with Talabat/Careem: Once merchants join, churn is <10%."

---

### **Q: "How do you prevent merchants from gaming the system?"**
**A:** "Three controls:

1. **Manual review Year 1:** Transactions >AED 50 require approval
2. **Velocity limits:** Max 5 redemptions/day per merchant
3. **Fraud scoring:** ML model flags suspicious patterns (same customer, same merchant, 10x/day)

Plus: Merchants have NO incentive to game it. They only pay when customer REDEEMS. If they fake transactions, they're paying 15% for nothing."
