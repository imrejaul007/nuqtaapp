## üßÆ Key Assumptions & Drivers

### 1. **Take Rate: 7.5%**
**How we earn:**
- Merchant commission: 5% on GMV (base)
- Promoted listings: +1.5% (25% of merchants pay extra for visibility)
- Bank partnerships: +0.5% (affiliate revenue from payment partners)
- Float interest: +0.5% (interest on wallet deposits)

**Why this is conservative:**
- Deliveroo/Uber Eats: 25-30% take rate
- Traditional credit cards: 2-3% per transaction
- Marketplace platforms: 10-20% (Airbnb, Booking.com)
- We're at **7.5%** - low enough to win merchants, high enough to scale

---

### 2. **User Growth Model**

**Year 1-2: Paid Acquisition**
- Campus ambassadors: AED 25-30 CAC
- QR code campaigns: AED 30-35 CAC
- Influencer partnerships: AED 35-40 CAC
- **Blended CAC: AED 30**

**Year 3-5: Organic + Network Effects**
- Word-of-mouth: 40% of new users (AED 10 CAC)
- Merchant-driven: 30% (merchants promote Nuqta to customers - AED 15 CAC)
- Paid: 30% (AED 40 CAC for cold audiences)
- **Blended CAC drops to AED 20-25**

**Retention Curve:**
- Month 1: 50% retention
- Month 3: 65% retention
- Month 6: 75% retention
- Month 12: 80% retention (power users locked in)

---

### 3. **Merchant Growth Model**

**Acquisition Strategy:**
- Year 1: Direct sales (expensive, AED 300 CAC)
- Year 2-3: Merchant referrals + inbound (AED 200 CAC)
- Year 4-5: Inbound only (AED 100-150 CAC) - brand pull

**Merchant Economics:**
- Average merchant generates AED 50K GMV/month
- Nuqta earns AED 3,750/month per merchant (7.5% √ó AED 50K)
- Merchant LTV (3 years): AED 135K ($36.8K)
- Merchant CAC: AED 300 ($82)
- **LTV:CAC ratio: 150x** (this is why merchants love us)

**Churn:**
- Year 1-2: 30% annual churn (early adopters testing)
- Year 3-5: 10-15% annual churn (product-market fit)

**Why users transact more over time:**
