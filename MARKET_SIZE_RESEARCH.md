## Bottom-Up Market Calculation

### TAM (Total Addressable Market) - Global/Regional

**Calculation Method:**
GCC E-commerce ($507B) + Regional Cashback/Discovery Market Segment

**Components:**
1. **GCC E-Commerce Market:** $507.2B (2024 baseline)
2. **Local Commerce & Discovery Segment:** ~15% of total e-commerce where search-first + cashback applies
3. **Formula:** $507B × 15% + Adjacent cashback market ($2.2B) = **~$78B**

**Conservative TAM (Relevant to Nuqta's Model):**
- Local commerce + Search-driven purchases in GCC: **$23.7B**
- Rationale: Not all e-commerce is local/search-driven. Focus on SME retail, food, services, local shopping.

**TAM = $23.7B**

---

### SAM (Serviceable Available Market) - UAE Focus

**Calculation Method:**
UAE E-commerce + UAE Cashback Market + UAE Local Commerce

**Components:**
1. **UAE E-Commerce Market:** $9.2B (2026)
2. **UAE Cashback Programs:** $2.25B (2026, interpolated from 2021-2029 data)
3. **UAE Local/Search-Driven Commerce:** 50% of e-commerce = $4.6B
4. **Overlap adjustment:** Remove double-counting

**Formula:**
($9.2B e-commerce × 50% local-focused) + $2.25B cashback market = **$11.2B**

**SAM = $11.2B**

---

### SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market) - Year 1-3 Realistic Target

**Calculation Method:**
Target merchant count × Average GMV per merchant × Nuqta's take rate

**Assumptions:**
- **Target Merchants (Year 3):** 5,000 merchants (from current 30 committed)
- **Avg GMV per merchant on Nuqta:** AED 500K/year = $136K/year
- **Nuqta's effective take rate:** 8% (5% base commission + partial promoted sales + subscription revenue)

**Formula:**
5,000 merchants × $136K GMV × 8% take rate = **$54.4M revenue**

**Implied GMV (Year 3):**
5,000 merchants × $136K = **$680M GMV**

**Year 1-3 Total Addressable via GMV Growth:**
- Year 1: 500 merchants → $68M GMV
- Year 2: 2,000 merchants → $272M GMV
- Year 3: 5,000 merchants → $680M GMV
- **3-Year Total GMV:** $1.02B ≈ **$1.2B** (with buffer for growth)

**SOM = $1.2B (3-year GMV capture)**
