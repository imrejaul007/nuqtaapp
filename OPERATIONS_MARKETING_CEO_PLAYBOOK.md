# PART 1: MARKETING STRATEGY

## 1.1 Marketing Philosophy

**Core Principle: Zero Paid Ads Until PMF**

We do NOT spend on paid advertising until we hit:
- 5,000 MAU (Monthly Active Users)
- 35% D30 Retention
- <5% Merchant Churn

**Why?** Paid ads without product-market fit = burning money. Our merchants ARE our marketing channel.

---

## 1.2 Marketing Channels (Priority Order)

### CHANNEL 1: Merchant QR Distribution (Primary) - 60% of Users
**Cost: AED 0 | CAC: AED 0**

Every merchant is a billboard:
- 500 merchants √ó 500 daily impressions = 250,000 daily impressions
- 2% scan rate = 5,000 potential signups/day
- Conversion to active user: 30% = 1,500 new users/day at scale

**Execution:**
1. Premium QR placement at checkout (eye-level, well-lit)
2. Table tents at every table (F&B)
3. Counter stands at reception (Salons, Gyms)
4. Staff verbal prompt: "Scan for 10% cashback"
5. Receipt footer: "Get rewards ‚Üí Scan QR"

**QR Design Requirements:**
- Nuqta branding (gold #c9a227 on dark #0a1628)
- Clear value prop: "Scan ‚Üí Get 10% Back"
- Size: Minimum 10cm √ó 10cm
- Materials: Acrylic stand, vinyl sticker, or framed poster

## 2.4 Merchant Operations

### Settlement Process (Net-7)

**Day 1-7:** Transactions accumulate
**Day 8:** Settlement calculated
**Day 9:** Merchant receives report via WhatsApp
**Day 10:** Bank transfer initiated
**Day 11-12:** Funds received by merchant

**Settlement Report Template:**
```

üè¶ Transfer initiated to: [Bank] ****1234
Expected arrival: [Date]

Questions? WhatsApp: +971-XXX-XXXX
```

### Merchant Support Tiers

**Tier 1: Self-Service (24/7)**
- FAQ on merchant portal
- Video tutorials
- Automated status updates

**Tier 2: WhatsApp Support (8AM-8PM)**
- Response SLA: 4 hours
- Issues: Settlement queries, profile updates, technical issues

**Tier 3: Account Manager (Business hours)**
- For Gold/Platinum merchants only
- Dedicated support line
- Monthly business reviews

### Merchant Health Scoring

**Score Components (100 points):**
- Transaction volume (30 points): <5/week = 0, 5-10 = 15, >10 = 30
- Settlement on time (20 points): Always = 20, Sometimes = 10, Never = 0
- Profile completeness (15 points): Photos, menu, hours
- Customer ratings (15 points): <3.5 = 0, 3.5-4 = 10, >4 = 15
- Response time (10 points): <4hr = 10, <24hr = 5, >24hr = 0
- UGC generated (10 points): 0 = 0, 1-5 = 5, >5 = 10

**Health Score Actions:**
- Score 80-100: Feature in "Popular Merchants"
- Score 60-79: Normal operations
- Score 40-59: Outreach to improve
- Score <40: Risk of churn, CEO intervention

## 3.6 CEO Founder Wellbeing

### Non-Negotiables

1. **Sleep:** 7+ hours/night
2. **Exercise:** 3x/week minimum
3. **Day off:** 1 full day/week (no email)
4. **Social:** 1 non-work dinner/week
5. **Learning:** 1 book/month

### Burnout Prevention Signals

**Warning Signs:**
- Dreading Monday mornings
- Snapping at team/family
- Decision paralysis
- Insomnia or oversleeping
- Losing passion for the mission

**Recovery Protocol:**
1. Take 3-day weekend (minimum)
2. Talk to mentor/therapist
3. Re-read "why I started"
4. Delegate more, do less
5. Set stricter boundaries

# PART 6: RISK MANAGEMENT

## 6.1 Key Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Merchant churn >10% | Medium | High | Weekly check-ins, quick settlement |
| User retention <20% | Medium | High | Gamification, better rewards |
| Cash runway <6mo | Low | Critical | Monthly burn review, raise early |
| Key hire leaves | Medium | Medium | Document everything, cross-train |
| Competitor launch | High | Medium | Speed, relationships, differentiation |
| Regulatory issue | Low | High | Legal advisor, proactive compliance |
| Tech failure | Low | High | Backups, monitoring, incident plan |

## 6.2 Contingency Plans

### If Merchant Growth Stalls
1. Increase commission to merchants (short-term)
2. Referral bonus for merchant-to-merchant
3. Pivot to event-only model temporarily
4. Partner with delivery platforms

### If User Growth Stalls
1. Increase signup bonus
2. Aggressive referral incentives
3. Influencer partnerships
4. Paid ads (if PMF proven)

### If Funding Falls Through
1. Cut team to founder only
2. Reduce marketing to zero
3. Focus on revenue, not growth
4. Bridge with angel investors
