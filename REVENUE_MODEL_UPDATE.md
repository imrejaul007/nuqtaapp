# Revenue Model Update - January 24, 2026

## Summary
Updated Business Model (Slide 9) to reflect the actual 4 revenue sources.

---

## ✅ New Revenue Model

### **4 Revenue Sources:**

1. **Commission on Sales (5%)**
   - Transaction commission from merchants on completed sales
   - Core revenue stream from platform transactions

2. **Promoted Sales (5%)**
   - Additional revenue when merchants promote their listings
   - Premium placement and featured deals

3. **Business Subscription (10 AED/user/month)**
   - Monthly subscription from shoppers
   - 10 AED per active user
   - Recurring revenue stream

4. **Affiliate Commission (Variable)**
   - Revenue share from bank partnerships
   - Payment partner commissions
   - Strategic partnerships

### **Merchant Side:**
- 5% commission on sales

- Benefits: 75-85% lower CAC vs Google Ads

- 10 AED monthly subscription
- Earn 5-10% cashback in Nuqta Coins
- Access to local deals

### **Platform Side:**

- Affiliate revenue (banks, payment partners)
