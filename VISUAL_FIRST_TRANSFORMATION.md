## ğŸ¨ VISUAL HIERARCHY

### Typography Scale
- **Hero number:** 7xl-9xl (72-128px) - IMPOSSIBLE TO MISS
- **Secondary numbers:** 5xl (48px) - Easy to scan
- **Labels:** sm (14px) - Context only
- **Title:** 2xl-3xl (24-30px) - Standard

### Color Hierarchy
- **Red numbers:** Problems (negative)
- **Green numbers:** Solutions (positive)
- **White numbers:** Neutral facts
- **Gray labels:** Supporting text

### Spacing Hierarchy
- **Hero section:** 12 units margin (mb-12)
- **Between cards:** 8 units gap (gap-8)
- **Inside cards:** 4 units padding (p-4)
