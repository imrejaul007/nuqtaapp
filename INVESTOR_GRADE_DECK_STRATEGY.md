## AS AN EXPERIENCED INVESTOR, HERE'S WHAT MAKES A 10/10 DECK:

### 1. **IMMEDIATE CLARITY** (First 30 seconds)
- I know exactly what you do
- I see the market size
- I understand why now

### 2. **PAIN SO CLEAR IT HURTS** (Problem slides)
- Quantified pain (not "users struggle" but "users lose AED 150/month")
- Both sides of marketplace pain (user AND merchant)
- Makes me think "this HAS to exist"

### 3. **UNFAIR ADVANTAGE OBVIOUS** (Solution slides)
- Not just "we do X better"
- But "we have Y that nobody else can replicate"
- Network effects, data moat, or technical barrier clear

### 4. **NUMBERS THAT MAKE SENSE** (Business slides)
- Unit economics aren't aspirational - they're based on real behavior
- CAC isn't "we'll figure it out" - it's tested across channels
- LTV isn't guessed - it's modeled with retention curves

### 5. **DE-RISKED EXECUTION** (Traction slides)
- Show, don't tell
- Contracts > MOUs
- Product screenshots > wireframes
- Team assembled > hiring plans

### 6. **CLEAR PATH TO 10X** (Growth slides)
- Not "we'll grow" but "here's exactly how"
- Multiple channels, not single-threaded
- Realistic milestones

### 7. **FOUNDER CREDIBILITY** (Team slide)
- Why YOU specifically
- What unfair advantages you have
- Evidence you can execute
